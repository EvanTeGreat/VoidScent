Welcome to Voidsent SMP!

Join us on Voidsent, a survival Minecraft server where you can explore, build, and survive together. Hop in at play.voidsent.net to start your adventure!

Discord: discord.voidsent.net

Server Features

Our server offers a balanced survival experience with land claims, custom enchants, and items, allowing you to protect your builds and explore vast collections of items with a simple yet effective plugin. We have a custom economy system, player shops, and a variety of biomes to explore. Plus, our active staff ensures smooth gameplay with regular updates and events to keep things engaging!

Rules

1. No hacking of any kind.

2. No doxing or leaking other players' information.

3. No excessive use of derogatory language.

4. No griefing or stealing.

5. No duplication machines.

6. No spamming in chat or using inappropriate language.

7. No border claiming or destroying areas near other players' claims.

8. Respect all players and staff members.

9. Glitches should be reported, not abused.

10. Staff have the final say.

11. Use common sense.
