Welcome to Voidsent SMP!

Join us on Voidsent, a survival Minecraft server where you can explore, build, and survive together. Hop in at play.voidsent.net to start your adventure!

Server Features

Our server offers a balanced survival experience with land claims, allowing you to protect your builds with a simple yet effective protection plugin. We have a custom economy system, player shops, and a variety of biomes to explore. Plus, our active staff ensures smooth gameplay with regular updates and events to keep things engaging!

Rules

1. No griefing or destroying other players' builds.

2. Respect all players—no harassment, cheating, or exploiting bugs.

3. No spamming in chat or using inappropriate language.

4. Use common sense and follow the server's guidelines.

Apply for Moderator

Requirements:

1. At least 50 hours played on the server.

2. Active and mature, with a clear understanding of the server rules.

3. Must be willing to help players and enforce server rules in a fair and consistent manner.

4. Previous moderation experience is a plus.

Apply for Builder

Requirements:

1. Must have experience building in Minecraft (screenshots or portfolio required).

2. Ability to create aesthetically pleasing and functional builds.

3. Willingness to collaborate with other builders and staff.

4. Must be active and communicate well with the team.

Apply for Developer

Requirements:

1. Experience with Minecraft server management and plugins.

2. Proficient in Java, Spigot, or Paper API.

3. Ability to troubleshoot and fix bugs or issues as they arise.

4. Must be reliable and committed to improving the server’s technical aspects.

5. Prior experience with server development is preferred but not required.
